# SLA Contract — QuickMart Retail Pvt Ltd
**Contract ID:** SLA-QM-2026-001
**Effective:** 1 January 2026
**Counterparty:** Sigma DataTech Pvt Ltd

## Service Level Commitments

### Data Pipeline Availability
Sigma DataTech guarantees that merchant transaction data will be available
in the reporting dashboard within 15 minutes of transaction completion,
with 99.5% uptime measured monthly.

### GMV Loss Threshold
In the event of a data pipeline failure, Sigma DataTech's liability
is triggered when the GMV of unloaded transactions exceeds:

**₹50,000 (Fifty Thousand Indian Rupees)** in any rolling 4-hour window.

If this threshold is breached:
1. Sigma DataTech must notify QuickMart's technical contact within 2 hours
   of detection.
2. A written root cause analysis must be delivered within 24 hours.
3. A service credit of 0.5% of the monthly platform fee is applied for
   each additional hour of breach beyond the initial 4 hours.
